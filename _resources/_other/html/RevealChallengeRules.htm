During each Uncover the Music game, Players will be asked to match pairs of tiles. The more matched pairs that a Player reveals, the more decibels he/she can earn.

  3. HOW TO PARTICIPATE IN THE UNCOVER THE MUSIC SAN GAME: There will be various Uncover the Music games going on at the same time. Each Uncover the Music game has a "room" which you must "enter" in order to play that game. To participate in an Uncover the Music game, Players must first enter one of the rooms by clicking on the link for that room. Players can view how many Players are in each room and the V-EGO (screen name) of each Player prior to entering a room. There must be a minimum of 3 Players in a room to start a game, with a maximum of 5 Players per game. The first Player that entered the room decides when to start the game (once there are at least 3 Players in the room, the first Player has 30 seconds to decide when to start the game).

    The Player to take the first turn will be randomly selected, and Players will continue in a counterclockwise manner. An individual game will include a 24 square grid with 11 matching pairs randomly positioned. During each turn, a Player will click on up to two tiles from the grid. A Player is given 15 seconds to click on up to two tiles; if both tiles are not clicked within this time frame, Player will forfeit his/her turn. After a tile is clicked, it will reveal a pattern that corresponds to a musical accompaniment. (Be sure to turn the volume up on your speakers!) If the patterns on the tiles and their musical accompaniment exactly match, that Player is awarded 1 decibel. Each time a pair of tiles is matched, those tiles are removed from the grid. If a Player matches a pair of tiles, he/she will continue to click up to two more tiles until a match is no longer revealed. Game play will continue until ALL pairs have been matched. Each grid will also include one Wild Card and one Pass Card. (The first time a Wild or Pass Card is revealed, it will be removed from the grid.) The Wild Card allows you to match with any other card. If a Player clicks the Pass Card as the first tile, he/she will not click a second tile, and the next Player in a counterclockwise position begins their turn. If a Player clicks the Pass Card as the second tile, the next Player in a counterclockwise position begins their turn and the first tile remains on the board. If a Player does not reveal a match, the next Player in a counterclockwise position begins their turn to click up to two tiles. The winner is the Player with the highest number of matching pairs in a game and will receive an additional bonus 60 decibels. In the event two or more Players are tied for the most matching pairs at the conclusion of a game, the bonus 60 decibels will be equally split among all tied Players. Decibel account holders can play an Uncover the Music game as many times as they like, however, the maximum number of decibels an account holder can receive playing Uncover the Music is 300 per day (12 midnight - 11:59 p.m.). All decibels earned during an Uncover The Music game will be automatically added to Player's Decibel account immediately upon game completion. In the event a game ends early as a result of Player(s) dropping out thereby reducing the total number of Players to fewer than 3, the game is terminated and all decibels earned at that point will be credited immediately. If a game ends early due to Player dropouts, or for any other reason, the bonus 60 decibels will NOT be awarded.
